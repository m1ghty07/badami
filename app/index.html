About Us

The setting is splendid, the hills enclosing the plains can be seen from afar over which looms two rocky rust red sandstone spurs on which the Chalukyas built their fortress. Badami a small temple town also known as Vatapi where the Chalukya’s capital stood in the sixth century under the glory of Pulakeshi II, described as the prosperous town. It’s strong army with hundreds of war elephants and the splendid monuments.
